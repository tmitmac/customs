Travelling to Square Country

What to do before you travel to Square Country and what to expect when you arrive.
Before you travel to Square Country ▼

Things to do before you travel to Square Country

Check your passport is valid. Check its expiry date to make sure it meets the rules for acceptable travel documents.

Check your eligibility to travel to Square Country and if you require a visa or SCeTA.

Check what you can and can’t bring into Square Country.

Square Country Travellers Declaration ▼

Everyone travelling to Square Country must complete a Square Country Travellers Declaration (SCTD).

It's free and needs to be completed before reaching passport control in Square Country.

You can complete a declaration on the Square Country Customs Website or by downloading the SCTD app.

To make your arrival easier do your digital declaration before you board your flight to Square Country.

It's perfectly reasonable if you cannot complete a declaration online. Instead, you will need to complete a paper declaration form. This form will be available on arrival in Square Country.

You need to answer questions about your trip and what you’re bringing into the country.

To complete a declaration or to find out more, visit www.TravellerDeclaration.gov.sc.
